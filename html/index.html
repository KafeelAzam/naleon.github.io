USE OF MULTIVITAMINS

Multivitamin tablets contain a combination of vitamins and minerals that are essential for overall health and well-being. Here are some common uses of multivitamin tablets.

Nutrient deficiency

Multivitamin tablets can help replenish any nutrient deficiencies in the body that may occur due to poor dietary habits, certain medical conditions, or specific diets such as vegan or vegetarian.

Promoting general health

Multivitamin tablets can help promote general health and well-being by supporting immune function, maintaining healthy skin and hair, and improving overall energy levels.

Pregnancy

Multivitamin tablets are often recommended for pregnant women to support the healthy growth and development of the baby.

Aging

As we age, our bodies may become less efficient at absorbing nutrients from food, making multivitamin tablets a useful supplement to support overall health and well-being.

Specific medical conditions

Multivitamin tablets may be recommended as a supplement for individuals with specific medical conditions, such as malabsorption syndromes, gastrointestinal disorders, or chronic diseases that may increase nutrient requirements.

SIDE EFFECTS

Some possible side effects of multivitamin tablets include upset stomach, nausea, diarrhea, headaches, and a metallic taste in the mouth.
